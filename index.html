About me

I am a third year PhD student at the Laboratoire de Mathématiques d’Orsay and a member of Inria Celeste team. I work under the supervision of Gilles Blanchard and Sylvain Arlot on sequential decision making and statistical learning theory. Prior to that, I graduated from Ecole Polytechnique with a focus in Applied Mathematics and obtained a Masters Degree in Statistics and Maching Learning from Paris-Saclay University.
