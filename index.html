About me

I am a post-doc in MISTEA Laboratory at INRAE.
I work on the ASCAI project led by Nicolas Vezelen and Alexandra Carpentier.
Prior to that, I was a Ph.D. candidate at Paris-Saclay University under the supervision of Gilles Blanchard and Sylvain Arlot.
My research focuses mainly on Bandit Theory and Active Learning.
