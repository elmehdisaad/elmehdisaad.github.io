About me

I am an Assistant Professor in the College of Computing at Mohammed VI Polytechnic University (UM6P). Before joining UM6P, I was a postdoctoral researcher in the OPTIMAL Lab at KAUST, where I worked with Francesco Orabona. Earlier, I held a postdoctoral position at Inrae Montpellier on the ASCAI project, led by Nicolas Verzelen and Alexandra Carpentier, and I also served as an Assistant Professor at the Laboratoire des Signaux et Systèmes at CentraleSupélec. I obtained my PhD in 2022 from the Laboratoire de Mathématiques d’Orsay at Paris-Saclay University, working with Gilles Blanchard and Sylvain Arlot, after completing my engineering studies at École Polytechnique. My research focuses on online learning, bandit algorithms, and theoretical machine learning.
